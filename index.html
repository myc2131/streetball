FIBA 3X3 Rules Revision

3X3 (pronounced 3 on 3) basketball is a form of the game played three a side on one hoop and it is the largest urban team sport of the world. This basketball discipline is currently being promoted and structured by International Basketball Federation (FIBA). Now they have an annual FIBA 3X3 World Tour which becomes the offical tournament of street basketball.
